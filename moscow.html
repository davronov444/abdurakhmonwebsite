MOSCOW 
 Russia, officially known as the Russian Federation, is the largest country in the world by land area, covering approximately 17.1 million square kilometers.** It is located in Eastern Europe and northern Asia, and spans nine time zones, making it one of the most geographically diverse countries in the world. Russia is home to a population of approximately 145 million people, making it the ninth most populous country in the world. The capital city of Russia is Moscow, which is also its most populous city with a population of over 12 million people. The official language of Russia is Russian, which is spoken by the vast majority of the population. Russia has a rich and complex history, with its roots going back to the 9th century when it was first formed as a collection of principalities. Over the centuries, Russia has grown into a major global power, with significant influence in politics, economics, and culture.Today, Russia is known for its diverse landscapes, from the Ural Mountains and Siberian taiga forests to its vibrant cities and historic sites. It is home to a wide range of ethnic groups and cultures, and has a rich cultural heritage that includes literature, music, art, and architecture.
